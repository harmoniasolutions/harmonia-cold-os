Logo Concept Exploration — 6 Directions

01 — Infinity Airflow

Continuous loop with airflow tapering. Represents 24/7 always-on automation. Clean, modern, embroiders well.

02 — Overlapping Circles

Three systems (Voice, CRM, Scheduling) converging at a harmony point. Minimal and versatile at any size.

03 — Triquetra Knot

Celtic triple knot representing three pillars woven together. Premium feel, strong brand mark.

04 — Dual Wave

Two waves in harmony — voice + airflow. Musical harmony meets HVAC. Simple and iconic.

05 — Möbius Strip

Twisted continuous loop, no beginning or end. Represents seamless lead flow. Distinctive 3D feel.

06 — Golden Spiral

Fibonacci growth curve. Natural harmony and expansion. Suggests leads flowing in and growing.
