FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Do you take walk-ins?
A: Yes, walk-ins are welcome based on availability. However, we recommend booking an appointment to guarantee your preferred time.

Q: How long does a typical haircut take?
A: A women's haircut typically takes 30-45 minutes. A men's haircut is about 20-30 minutes. Children's haircuts are about 15-20 minutes.

Q: How long does hair coloring take?
A: Full color takes about 1.5-2 hours. Highlights take about 2-3 hours. Balayage can take 2.5-3.5 hours depending on length and desired result.

Q: Do I need a consultation for color services?
A: For first-time color clients, we recommend a brief consultation. You can discuss this during your appointment or call ahead.

Q: What forms of payment do you accept?
A: We accept cash, all major credit cards, and Apple Pay.

Q: Is there parking available?
A: Yes, there is street parking available on Main Street and a public lot nearby.

Q: Do you offer gift cards?
A: Yes! Gift cards are available in any amount at the salon.

Q: What products do you use/sell?
A: We use and sell professional-grade hair care products. Ask your stylist for personalized product recommendations.

Q: Can I bring my child to my appointment?
A: Children are welcome. For safety reasons, children must be supervised by another adult if they are not receiving services.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: We require 24-hour notice for cancellations. No-shows and same-day cancellations may be subject to a cancellation fee.
